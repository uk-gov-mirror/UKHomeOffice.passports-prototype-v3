How long it takes

It takes up to 3 weeks to renew or replace a passport online. It may take longer for a first adult passport.

You may be able to get a passport urgently to travel for compassionate reasons or work, or to prove your identity.

Do not book travel until you have a valid passport - your new passport will not have the same number as your old one.

Before you start

You’ll need a debit or credit card to use this service.

It’s £9.50 cheaper to apply for a passport online than by post.
