Who can do this

You need to ask someone who:

  • is 18 or older
  • and lives in the UK
  • and has a current UK passport
  • and works in or is retired from a recognised profession

If they have an Irish passport you will need to ask them to use a paper form to confirm your identity.

They must have known you personally for at least 2 years, for example as a friend, neighbour or colleague.

{% call hmpoDetails({ summaryText: "If they can’t do this online" }) %}

You need to ask them to use a paper form. The person needs a current UK or Irish passport.

{% endcall %}

Who can’t do this

You can’t ask someone to confirm your identity if you’re:

  • related to them by birth or marriage
  • in a relationship with or live at the same address as them

They can’t work at HM Passport Office or some areas of UK Visas and Immigration.
