Urgent travel for compassionate reasons, or government business

Contact the Passport Office to find out what to do if you need a passport urgently for any of the following reasons:

  • the death of, or serious illness of a family member or a friend
  • urgent medical treatment that cannot be delayed
  • national government business, for example Members of Parliament, HM forces, officials in the Diplomatic service or Departments of State
  • local government business, for example police officers, local councillors, or social services staff where travel with children in care is involved

We may ask you for evidence to support your urgent application for a passport.

Identification purposes

We are giving priority to those who need a passport urgently for compassionate reasons, or government business.

You may be able to use an expired passport as evidence of your identity. Check first with the organisation you need to confirm identity with.

If you still have an urgent need for a passport for identification purposes contact the Passport Office.
